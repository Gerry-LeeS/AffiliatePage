About Casino Site Reviews

Last updated: May 2026

Casino Site Reviews is an independent UK casino review site dedicated to helping players find safe, fair, and genuinely enjoyable online casinos. Who We Are

Casino Site Reviews was founded in 2025 by a team of UK-based gambling enthusiasts with years of experience playing at and evaluating online casinos. We got tired of affiliate sites that recommended anything and everything regardless of quality — so we built something different.

Our editorial team — known collectively as the CSR Analysts — brings together expertise in online gaming, UK gambling regulation, bonus mathematics, and consumer protection. Every casino we review is assessed hands-on, not just based on publicly available information. We open real accounts, test real withdrawals, and contact real customer support teams before we publish a word.

We are an affiliate website, which means we may earn a commission when you sign up to a casino through one of our links. This is how we fund the site. However, our commercial relationships never influence our ratings or recommendations — if a casino doesn't meet our standards, it doesn't appear on the site, regardless of the commission on offer. Our 7-Point Review Framework

Every casino on Casino Site Reviews is rated across the same 7-point framework. This gives you a consistent, comparable score for every operator — and means you always know exactly what went into the rating.

The CSR 7-Point Framework

  • 1. Welcome Bonus: We assess the headline offer, wagering requirements, maximum withdrawal limits, eligible games, and overall fairness of the bonus terms. A big number means nothing with 60x wagering attached.
  • 2. Game Selection: We evaluate the breadth and quality of the game library — slots, live tables, jackpots, game shows — and the reputation of the software providers behind them.
  • 3. Payout Speed: We test how quickly withdrawals are processed across different payment methods. Fast payouts are a strong indicator of a trustworthy, well-run operation.
  • 4. Payment Methods: We check the range of deposit and withdrawal options available, including whether e-wallets, Apple Pay and Instant Banking are supported.
  • 5. Mobile Experience: We test each casino on mobile browsers and, where available, dedicated apps — assessing performance, layout, and ease of use on smaller screens.
  • 6. Security & Licensing: We verify each operator's UKGC licence status, SSL encryption, and data protection standards. Unlicensed casinos are never listed, no exceptions.
  • 7. Responsible Gambling: We check for deposit limits, loss limits, session timers, self-exclusion tools, and GamStop integration. Player safety is non-negotiable.

Our Editorial Standards

Every review on this site is written and edited by the CSR team based on direct experience with the casino. We update our reviews regularly — bonuses change, withdrawal times shift, and new games launch. If something changes at a casino we cover, we update the review to reflect it. All review pages display a "Last Updated" date so you always know how recent the information is.

We do not publish sponsored reviews, paid placements, or "advertorial" content. If a casino pays for promotion, we disclose it clearly. Our star ratings are never adjusted based on commercial relationships. A casino that pays us affiliate commission gets exactly the same honest treatment as one that doesn't.

Why Only UKGC Licensed Casinos?

The UK Gambling Commission is one of the strictest and most respected gambling regulators in the world. Every casino holding a UKGC licence must meet rigorous requirements for fair play, secure banking, transparent bonus terms, and robust responsible gambling tools. Players at UKGC licensed casinos are protected by UK law — including the right to raise complaints with the regulator if something goes wrong.

We made a deliberate decision from day one to only ever list UKGC licensed operators. Offshore and grey-market casinos may offer bigger bonuses, but they come with significantly less player protection. We think that's a trade-off our readers should never have to make.

Responsible Gambling

We take responsible gambling seriously. Every review on this site includes information about the responsible gambling tools available at that casino. We link to GamStop, GamCare and BeGambleAware across the site — and if you or someone you know is struggling with gambling, we encourage you to reach out to one of these organisations for support.

All content on Casino Site Reviews is intended for adults aged 18 and over. Gambling should be entertaining — if it stops feeling that way, please use the tools available to take a break.
