How We Rank Casino Bonuses

Not all casino bonuses are created equal. A headline figure of "200 Free Spins" sounds impressive, but what matters is the wagering requirement attached to any winnings, the time limit for completing those requirements, the game restrictions that apply, and — most importantly — the realistic chance you have of actually withdrawing money from the offer. Our ranking looks at all of these factors together, not just the headline number.

We give particular weight to wagering requirements because they are the single biggest factor in determining real-world bonus value. Our Bonus Scoring Methodology

A large headline number — "500 free spins!" — means very little without understanding the wagering requirement attached to any winnings. We calculate the realistic expected value of each bonus after accounting for the playthrough multiplier, the game weighting (slots typically count 100%, but live casino games may count 10% or less), and any maximum win cap on free spin winnings.

Our ranking heavily weights wagering requirements. A 0x wagering bonus from PlayOJO is worth more to most players than a 200-spin offer with a 65x playthrough requirement, because the latter is statistically almost impossible to clear. We also factor in minimum deposit amount, withdrawal speed, and whether the casino uses sticky or non-sticky bonus mechanics.
