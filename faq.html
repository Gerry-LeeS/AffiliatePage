What are wagering requirements?

▼

Wagering requirements (also called playthrough requirements) specify how many times you must bet your bonus before you can withdraw winnings.

Example: If you receive a £100 bonus with 30x wagering requirements, you must place £3,000 in bets (£100 × 30) before you can withdraw any winnings from that bonus.

Tips:

  • Lower wagering requirements are better (20x–30x is reasonable)
  • Check which games contribute toward requirements (slots usually count 100%)
  • Read the full terms before accepting any bonus

What is KYC verification and why is it required?

▼

KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is a legal requirement for all UK-licensed casinos. It involves confirming your identity, age, and address by submitting documents such as:

  • Photo ID (passport, driver's licence)
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
  • Sometimes: proof of payment method

Why it's required: KYC protects against underage gambling, money laundering, and fraud. It also ensures you can withdraw winnings smoothly.

Complete verification as soon as possible after registering to avoid delays when withdrawing.
