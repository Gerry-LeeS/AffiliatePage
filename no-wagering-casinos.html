What Are No Wagering Casinos?

No wagering casinos — also called wager-free casinos or 0x wagering casinos — are online gambling sites where bonus winnings are paid directly as real, withdrawable cash. There are no playthrough conditions attached. When you win from a wager-free free spin, that money is yours immediately, with no strings attached.

This stands in sharp contrast to the majority of casino bonuses in the UK, where free spin winnings or bonus funds must be wagered a certain number of times (commonly 30x to 65x) before any withdrawal is permitted. A £5 win from free spins with 35x wagering means you would need to bet £175 before you can cash out a single pound. With no wagering, that same £5 win goes straight into your withdrawable balance.

The practical value of wager-free bonuses is significantly higher than their headline size suggests. Not all "no wagering" claims are equal. Some casinos advertise zero wagering on free spins but apply maximum win caps that limit how much you can actually withdraw. Others offer no-wagering bonuses only as a temporary promotion rather than a permanent platform policy. True no wagering casinos — like PlayOJO — apply zero playthrough requirements across the entire site, not just on selected offers.
